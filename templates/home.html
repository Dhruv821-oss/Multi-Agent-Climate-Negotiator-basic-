System Architecture

🌐 Multi-Agent System

Each agent represents a nation with distinct economic priorities, emission profiles, and strategic objectives.

📉 Climate-Economic Tradeoff

Agents balance short-term economic gain with long-term climate risk, leading to complex strategic behavior.

🔁 Adaptive Learning

Policies evolve dynamically through reinforcement learning, enabling emergent cooperation and competition.

Key Capabilities

🤝 Negotiation Protocol

Multi-round bargaining with counter-offers and deadlines.

🏛 Coalition Formation

Agents form alliances based on trust and aligned incentives.

⚡ Shock Modeling

External disruptions like disasters and economic collapse.

Analytics & Insights

📊 Explainability

Decompose rewards into interpretable components.

📈 Emergent Behavior

Observe cooperation, inequality, and stability patterns.

⚖️ Equilibrium Detection

Identify stable strategic configurations.

Research Impact

This platform serves as a computational laboratory for studying global coordination problems. It enables researchers to analyze policy outcomes, simulate interventions, and explore the dynamics of cooperation under uncertainty in complex systems.
